My journey:

In 2014, I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in business administration. My first professional job after university was working as a customer service representative at a well known multinational Bank in Cairo. A year later, I was offered a position to be working as a financial accountant in one of the leading real estate development companies in Egypt.
After working there for 2 years, I realized that my time at this company has come to an end and it was time for me to move on to the next chapter of my professional career. I had the urge to explore and learn more about the international market, and so I applied for an MBA in Germany (Masters of Business Administration) which I graduated from in 2019. My master’s studies specialized in International trade which I found extremely interesting.
During my master’s studies in Germany, I had a wonderful experience working in the marketing department for the Anhaltisches Theater in Dessau. Working there was a great opportunity for me to fully explore and comprehend the German market and the working environment and also allowed me to pursue a real case study for my master thesis.
On the other hand, Information Technology has always been a passion of mine. My obsession with IT has made me recognize how I have always been fascinated by the prospect of editing websites’ mark-up that aids in achieving marketing objectives, understanding the complex financial softwares, and even encouraged me to build my own PC for gaming purposes and other interests.
Finally, I realized that IT is the direction that I want to head to with my career. Fortunately, after obtaining my master’s degree, I was accepted as a 1s-level database service disk analyst, where my technological abilities widely expanded along with my experience and background knowledge. Tackling the day-to-day issues allowed me to understand the importance and beauty of web development.
Needless to say, my thirst for learning is limitless, and so I decided to learn coding and pursue my technological career in a well-appreciated field.
